Websites, Mobile sites and Apps: UX/UI Design & Development

A great website is never just about fancy-looking web pages. It is always a winning combination of user-friendly information architecture, intuitive and attractive designs, and well-written useful content – all of which work towards solving some definite brand and business problems and achieving some specific business and marketing objectives.
Great experience design creates an emotionally positive connection between the user and the brand, fosters loyalty, and allows you to define customer journeys that are most conducive to your business.



What’s the RezMatic Media way?

Over the years, we have tweaked, re-tweaked and refined our website development approach in order to arrive at a systematic and efficient process - one that has consistently produced the best results for us and our clients. Our approach integrates industry best practices with our own tried-and-tested processes developed over two decades of experience in website development.

Develop the core visual and verbal communication concept that's in sync with your brand and business objectives.

Create a website structure that can be efficiently crawled and indexed, and delivers optimal SERP rankings.

Apply the time-tested principles of messaging hierarchy, layout, typeface weight and pairing, and more, to communicate effectively.

You can tap into our capabilities to develop avant-garde UI and UX designs that are responsive and optimized for every digital screen out there. A customer centric and result oriented approach is undertaken to develop an ingenious and impactful website design to cater to your preferred audience.

Our landscape of oﬀerings


Corporate and Brand Websites

Users are increasingly turning to the internet to ﬁnd answers to their needs, and can distinguish a good website from a poor one. So it is important for businesses to improve their digital presence, and provide pertinent answers in an easy-to-ﬁnd-and-consume format.

Transaction Websites

A user-friendly interface is the focal point of a transaction website. If your users not able to locate, access, or perform a transaction eﬀortlessly, they will move on to a service that provides them a better experience.

Campaign and Product Microsites

Need a single short-term marketing portal for a campaign or product? It is important to have one that gives its visitors an impactful and memorable experience and thus outlives its online presence.

E-commerce Sites

Unlike popular opinion, every e-commerce site is not designed to replicate another. In an overcrowded market such as e-commerce, businesses need to stand out instead of blending in.

ATM Screens

User-centred design and simple, clean interfaces is what ATM screens are all about. Our objective is always to design a UI that the ATM users ﬁnd convenient and pleasing to use.

Mobile and Desktop Applications and Games

Right from conception to the execution, we design and develop Apps and Games, taking into account the nuances of diﬀerent platforms and devices.
